Dr. Intekhab Alam is a skilled orthopaedics and joint replacement surgeon in Lucknow with over 14 years of experience in treating joint and musculoskeletal conditions. He practices at Fast Track Orthocare, providing expert diagnosis and advanced surgical and non-surgical orthopaedic care. He completed his MBBS and MS (Ortho.) from Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), Aligarh, and later worked as a Registrar at Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, AMU, before gaining extensive experience at leading centres including Fortis Memorial Research Institute, Gurugram, Max Healthcare, Saket, New Delhi, and Apollo Hospital, Lucknow.
